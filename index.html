Video Game Sales Analysis


I used a data set from Kaggle containing the sales statistics of video games, including a game's release year, genre, publisher, and regional sales (North America, Europe, Japan, Other, and Global).

I processed and cleaned to explore three questions: does genre really impact sales, are games of the top publisher (Nintendo) popular regardless of genre, and is it possible to predict the total yearly sales for all games.

Python, Pandas, Data Visualization, Data Analysis, Hypothesis Testing, Regression Analysis
